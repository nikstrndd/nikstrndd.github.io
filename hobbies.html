DOTA 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game in which two teams of five players compete to collectively destroy a large structure defended by the opposing team known as the "Ancient", whilst defending their own.

Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends is a 5v5 MOBA for handheld devices that offers a variety of playable heroes, game modes, and intense, fast-paced gameplay. With controls optimized for touchscreen devices, players can quickly get set up to play a match against other players in real time.
